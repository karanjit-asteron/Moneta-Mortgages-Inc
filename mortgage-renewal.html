Your renewal is one of the most important financial decisions you'll make — yet most Canadians simply sign what their bank sends them. We change that.

Don't Just Sign and Return

When your mortgage term ends, your lender will send you a renewal offer — often at a rate well above what's available in the market. Studies consistently show that the majority of Canadian homeowners accept their lender's first renewal offer without negotiating or shopping around. This single decision can cost tens of thousands of dollars over the life of a mortgage.

At Moneta Mortgages Inc., we step in at renewal time to advocate for you. We shop your renewal across our network of 50+ lenders, negotiate on your behalf, and help you select the product that best serves your financial objectives for the next term and beyond.

The Renewal Opportunity

At renewal, you can switch lenders without incurring a prepayment penalty — making it the perfect time to reassess your mortgage strategy. This is your opportunity to:

Secure a Better Rate — Often 0.5%–1.0% lower than your lender's posted renewal offer.

Change Mortgage Type — Switch from variable to fixed (or vice versa) with no penalty at renewal.

Adjust Your Amortization — Reset the amortization schedule to better fit your current financial situation.

Access Your Equity — Add a HELOC or restructure to access equity built during your last term.

Consolidate Debt — Roll other debts into your renewed mortgage at a significantly lower interest rate.

Improve Prepayment Privileges — Negotiate for better annual lump-sum and payment increase options.

When Should You Start the Renewal Process?

We recommend reaching out 4–6 months before your renewal date. This gives us sufficient time to shop your file, negotiate effectively, and lock in a rate without the pressure of a looming deadline. Many lenders allow you to lock in a rate up to 120 days before your renewal date at no cost — we'll advise you on the right timing based on current rate trends.

Switching Lenders at Renewal

Switching lenders at renewal time has never been easier. In most cases, there are no legal fees or prepayment penalties, and many lenders offer cash-back incentives to cover any switching costs. Our team handles all the paperwork and coordination between institutions, ensuring a completely seamless transition for you.

Renewal Rate Guarantee

We guarantee to find you a rate equal to or better than your lender's renewal offer — or we'll tell you clearly and honestly that staying put makes sense. Our advice is always in your best interest, never commission-driven.
