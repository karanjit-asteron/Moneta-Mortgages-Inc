Mortgage Types We Offer

Every buyer's situation is unique. We work with a broad range of mortgage products to ensure we can match you with exactly the right solution:

Fixed-Rate Mortgages — Predictable payments, locked-in security.

Variable-Rate Mortgages — Flexibility to benefit from rate drops.

Open Mortgages — Prepay any amount, any time, without penalty.

Closed Mortgages — Lower rates with reasonable prepayment limits.

CMHC Insured — Low down payment options with mortgage default insurance.

Conventional Mortgages — 20%+ down payment, no insurance required.

Short-Term Mortgages — 1–3 year terms for maximum flexibility.

Long-Term Mortgages — 5-year and 10-year terms for rate certainty.

What You'll Need to Apply

Our advisors make the documentation process as straightforward as possible. Typically, you'll need proof of income (pay stubs, T4s, or NOAs for self-employed borrowers), proof of employment, bank statements, and identification. We'll provide you with a personalized checklist at your consultation.
