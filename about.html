Our Story

Driven by Results

Moneta Mortgages Inc. was founded on a simple belief: every Albertan deserves access to the best possible mortgage — not just the one their bank happens to offer.

Our Mission

Independent Advice.
Exceptional Results.

As independent mortgage brokers, we aren't beholden to any single lender's agenda. Our only commitment is to you — our client. We access a network of more than 50 banks, credit unions, trust companies, and private lenders to identify the mortgage product that truly serves your needs.

Since opening our doors in Edmonton, we've helped over 1,200 Albertan families and businesses secure financing — saving them an average of $9,400 over their mortgage term compared to their bank's initial offer.
